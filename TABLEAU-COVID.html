Decoding COVID-19 in India: Tableau Dashboard Analysis

COVID-19 Analysis in India

Introduction:

In this project, I delved into the intricacies of the COVID-19 situation in India, employing advanced analytics techniques and visualization tools to unravel key trends. This endeavour not only honed my analytical skills but also underscored the importance of data-driven decision-making in tackling global crises.

Goals:

The primary goal of this project was to provide a comprehensive and visually engaging analysis of the COVID-19 landscape in India. Through interactive Tableau dashboards, I aimed to convey nuanced information regarding age group susceptibility, state-wise case distribution, gender-specific impact, vaccine administration, and the geographic spread of testing facilities.

Outcome:

1. Age Group Details:

Illustrated age-specific vulnerability through a Donut chart, revealing that individuals aged 21 to 39 accounted for over 45% of confirmed cases.

2. Covid Cases by State:

Utilized a line chart to depict the distribution of cases across states, with Maharashtra leading at over 23 million cases and boasting the highest number of testing labs (39).

3. Gender Distribution:

Presented gender-based insights using a Donut chart, showcasing that males comprised 66.76% of confirmed cases compared to 33.24% for females.

4. Vaccine Administration:

Visualized the vaccination landscape with horizontal bar charts, highlighting CoviShield's dominance with over 61 billion doses administered.

5. First vs Second Dose:

Explored the balance between first and second vaccine doses through horizontal bar charts, indicating over 56 billion first doses administered compared to approximately 13.5 billion second doses.

6. ICMR Testing Labs:

Mapped the distribution of ICMR testing labs across states using a horizontal bar chart, identifying Maharashtra, Uttar Pradesh, and Tamil Nadu as hotspots with both high COVID cases and testing facilities.

This project not only demonstrates my proficiency in data analysis but also underscores my ability to transform raw information into actionable insights, fostering a deeper understanding of complex phenomena.
